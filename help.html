Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a new quiz?

Simply go to the "Dashboard" and click on "Menu" then select a quiz topic and difficulty level. Then click "Start Quiz" to begin.

Can I retake a quiz?

Yes, after submitting your quiz, you can retake it by selecting the same topic and difficulty again.

What happens if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it via email.
